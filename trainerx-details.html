Developemt Info

In this application, there are two distinct user roles: gym owners and admin. The gym owner has the responsibility of managing multiple gym trainers and customers within their gym(s), while the admin has oversight over all gym owners and freelance trainers across the platform.

For gym owners, their primary tasks include managing their team of gym trainers, assigning schedules, tracking client progress, handling memberships, and facilitating communication with customers. They have access to features tailored to the management of their specific gym operations, such as scheduling tools, client databases, payment processing, and performance tracking metrics.

On the other hand, the admin role entails broader responsibilities. Admins have access to the entire platform and are tasked with overseeing the activities of all gym owners and freelance trainers. They monitor the performance of individual gyms, ensure compliance with platform policies and standards, handle disputes or issues that arise between users, and provide support and assistance as needed. Additionally, admins manage user accounts, permissions, and system settings to maintain the overall functionality and security of the application.

Overall, the application serves as a comprehensive management solution for gym owners to efficiently run their businesses while providing the admin with the tools and oversight necessary to maintain the integrity and effectiveness of the platform as a whole.
