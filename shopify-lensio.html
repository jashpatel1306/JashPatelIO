Apps Features

Lensio is an automated prescription verification app that enables you to sell contact lenses to US consumers. We take care of all legal prescription requirements so you can focus on what you do best: serving your customers. US law requires that all contact lens sellers verify a patient's contact lens prescription. We automate this process and take it off your hands. Lensio provides peace of mind that you are compliant with strict federal regulations.

  • Peace of mind: Lensio takes care of regulatory compliance so you don't need to.
  • Saves Time and Money: Lensio is plug & play so you can start selling right away.
  • Ensure Accuracy: our contact lens database has up to date info on every product.
